A bot for christmas

Have you ever agonized over the thoughs of your own existence? Have you ever spent countless nights awake by the disgusting, crippled, mortal weakness of your flesh? Have you ever longed for those sweet sweet Christmas days?

Fear no longer! Krismas is here for you!

Who
Krismas is a fully automated twitter bot that tells you how many days left till Christmas.
How to use it
Make a tweet with the tag #krismas and krismas will respond about 15 seconds later with the days left till christmas!
